Identity, Values, and Strengths Exploration


Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

My biggest decision when I decided to live in NZ 3 years ago. I was borned and grew up in Japan. In Japan there is not much mixed culture, values and identities. They believe people doing the same thing are the greatest, too self-conscious and your work is the best priority. They do not like anything unique. I always felt not free and could not be myself. Since I came to NZ people like unique and so many values. People do what they like and their best priority is always their family. I love myself more than when I was in Japan. It is very fortunate I met a great partner and friends who are always by my side in NZ. Besides, I found my new goal influenced by them.



Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

Since I came to NZ I have met a lot of people from a variety of nationalities. It is not easy at all living in a different culture but it shows me a new world (in a good way and a bad way…haha). It’s fun for me. I love that people think you can start or learn something new at any age in NZ. In Japan not many people try something new especially if you are over 30 years old. I was influenced by NZ culture and I am challenging my new study at Dev Academy! I am 30 years old and I want to prove to people in Japan that you can challenge and succeed in a different country even if you are 30 years old!



Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.

I have very strong motivation to achieve my goals. I never give up what I want to or what I want to be. Just keep going for the archive with a positive mind like I can do that!!! I am sure my strengths help me in my new journey in the IT industry.



Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

I have been worrying about a language barrier. I may need more time to understand what people are talking or explaining to me than other people. However I have kept improving myself and my language skills. I am taking note when I find an unknown word and when I am not sure something I always confirm that my understanding is correct.



Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.

I tend to try to make a good environment at work.This good environment means communicating with colleagues and trying to know their personality and try to show my personality too. I believe it will lead us to a great collaborative relationship. Once I met a difficult colleague. He was a bit stubborn with his opinions and did not listen to others' opinions.I tried to talk to him and found our common hobby. When we faced an issue he tended to listen to me more and we found a compromise to solve the issue.
