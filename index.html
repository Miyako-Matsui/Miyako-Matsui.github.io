??What is Oyako don??

Oyakodon is consisting of chicken sautéed and then cooked in a Japanese broth, and then finished with egg and served over rice.
Oyako means parent and child in Japanese. The dish uses chicken and egg. That's why we call it Oyako don (bowl of rice).
